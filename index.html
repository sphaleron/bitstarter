Combine the mobility and flexibility of your RPi credit-card sized computer with some state-of-the-art DSP algorithms to create an ultimate effects processor. Choose from our wide selection of distortion, delay, chorus etc. effects, combined with advanced amplifier simulation, and forget about your jumble of cables with so-so connectors and quality degradation.

The package includes a pair of carefully designed AD/DA to feed the guitar signal to your RPi, as well as an SD card preloaded with a realtime-optimized, low-latency operating system running our effects processor. As a registered user you will receive updates in the future and can buy new effects for yout PiDal. The modular infrastructure even enables you to develop your effects with our toolkit.

FAQ

What do I get for the price?
A rugged case with built-in DA/AD connectors for interfacing with the Raspberry Pi, and a bootable (in RPi) SD card preloaded with the software and effects. Note that the Rpi itself is not included, but has to be bought separately.
How can I develop my own effects?
We provide libraries for interfacing with the software, with bindings for C, C++ and Python.
